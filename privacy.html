Privacy Policy

Simple, direct, and transparent privacy approach.

This privacy policy applies to Ice Fishing Master developed by Medi-Kam. It is written to be easy to understand for users and reviewers.

No data collection

We do not collect personal data, analytics data, device data, or usage data from users of the app.

No storage

We do not store user data on our servers or external databases because we do not collect such data.

No sharing

We do not sell, transfer, disclose, or share user data with third parties.

Detailed statement

Ice Fishing Master does not request or intentionally collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, precise location, payment information, contacts, or files. Because no personal data is collected, there is no user data to review, export, sell, or delete on our side.

If the app changes in the future in a way that affects privacy practices, this policy will be updated clearly.
